# Governance Maturity Model

This model describes progressive governance capability levels for ADAAD deployments.

## Level 1 — Basic replay validation
Baseline replay verification of governed mutation decisions.

## Level 2 — Deterministic mutation enforcement
Fail-closed replay and deterministic runtime boundaries enforced before execution.

## Level 3 — Constitution-level constraints
Constitution and policy controls become mandatory mutation gates.

## Level 4 — Federated governance
Cross-team/domain governance coordination with policy precedence controls.

## Level 5 — Cross-repo policy inheritance
Governed policy inheritance and consistency checks across repositories.

## Level 6 — Formal verification integration
Mathematical/spec-based validation integrated with governance controls for critical paths.
