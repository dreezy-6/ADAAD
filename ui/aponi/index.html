Aponi Governed Mutation Proposal Authoring

Authority boundary:

Submissions from this editor are queued as governed proposals and must pass constitutional and human/governor controls before any execution pathway can occur. Operators should treat this as a drafting interface, not an execution console.

Execution authority disclaimer:

Queue admission is not deployment approval. Accepted proposals may still be rejected by policy gates, tier escalation requirements, replay checks, and human review controls.
