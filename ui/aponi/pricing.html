Frequently asked questions

What is a "governance epoch"?

An epoch is one complete cycle of the ADAAD evolution loop: agents propose mutations, the constitutional linter evaluates them, the GovernanceGate makes a verdict, and the audit ledger records the result. Community orgs get 50 per month; Pro orgs get 500.

Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period. No lock-in, no cancellation fees.

What happens if I exceed my epoch quota?

The API returns a 429 with an upgrade URL. Your existing governance state, ledger, and audit trail are never affected — only new epoch runs are blocked until the next billing window or an upgrade.

How does federation work on Pro?

Pro includes 3 federation nodes: peer discovery via gossip protocol, epoch synchronisation, and cross-node fitness comparison. Enterprise unlocks unlimited nodes, the federated signal broker, and cross-node budget arbitration for globally distributed autonomous systems.

Is my governance data private?

Yes. Your constitutional rules, mutation history, and audit ledger are logically isolated per organisation. InnovativeAI LLC never accesses your governance data without explicit written consent for support purposes.

Do you offer annual billing or custom contracts?

Yes — annual billing available at 20% discount. Enterprise custom contracts (custom SLAs, volume pricing, procurement paperwork) available via enterprise@innovativeai.io.
